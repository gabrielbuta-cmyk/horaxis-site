From Raw Data to
Prevented Disruptions.

Four steps between your ERP data and actionable supply chain intelligence.

1

Connect Your ERP

Your existing SAP S/4HANA, Oracle ERP Cloud, or Microsoft Dynamics 365 connects via pre-built connectors. ProcureAI syncs purchase orders, supplier records, delivery history, and BOM structures. Everything deploys on-premise inside your network — no data leaves your infrastructure.

  • ✓ SAP RFC & OData, Oracle REST, Dynamics Dataverse
  • ✓ Bi-directional write-back for approved updates
  • ✓ Docker Compose on-premise deployment
2

Enrich with Live Intelligence

Eight external risk engines continuously scan real-world conditions that affect your supply chain. Geopolitical disruptions from GDELT, port weather from NOAA and Open-Meteo, vessel movements from AIS, commodity prices, waterway levels, and currency volatility — all scored and integrated automatically.

  • ✓ GDELT geopolitical disruption scanning
  • ✓ NOAA & Open-Meteo port weather for 20+ cities
  • ✓ AIS vessel tracking across major shipping lanes
  • ✓ Metals, fuel, natural gas & forex monitoring
3

Predict & Prioritize

A 35-variable ML model combines your ERP delivery history with live external signals to predict late deliveries 14+ days in advance. Each purchase order gets a risk score, predicted delay in days, and explainable risk factors — so your team knows exactly what to act on and why.

  • ✓ Two-model ensemble: Random Forest + Gradient Boosting
  • ✓ 35 variables: supplier patterns, external signals, operational context
  • ✓ Explainable risk factors for every prediction
4

Act Before Impact

Recursive BOM traceability shows exactly which finished goods and customer orders are at risk when a delivery is late. Trace the full chain: raw material shortage, through BOM levels, to affected sales orders. Engage suppliers directly through secure portal links — no login required for them.

  • ✓ 10-level recursive BOM explosion
  • ✓ Customer orders at risk with value impact
  • ✓ Secure supplier links with SHA-256 token hashing
