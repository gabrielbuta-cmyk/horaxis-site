Data Processing Addendum (DPA)

This Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") governs the processing of personal data in connection with ProcureAI services provided by Horaxis.

1. Roles of the Parties

The Customer acts as Data Controller. Horaxis acts as Data Processor.

2. Purpose of Processing

Processing occurs solely to provide procurement intelligence, analytics, reporting, and workflow automation services.

3. Categories of Data

Data may include business contact details, supplier information, operational procurement records, and system-generated analytics.

4. Security Measures

  • Encrypted data transmission (TLS)
  • Role-based access control
  • Secure hosting environment
  • Operational monitoring

5. Subprocessors

Horaxis may engage infrastructure and service providers strictly for operational delivery purposes. Subprocessors are bound by appropriate data protection obligations.

6. Data Subject Rights

Horaxis supports GDPR rights including access, correction, restriction, portability, and deletion where applicable.

7. International Transfers

Where data is transferred internationally, appropriate safeguards are applied in accordance with GDPR requirements.

8. Term and Termination

This DPA remains in effect for the duration of the service agreement.

For questions regarding this DPA, contact info@horaxis.com.
