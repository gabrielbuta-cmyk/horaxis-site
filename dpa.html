Data Processing Addendum (DPA)

This DPA is incorporated into the Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") between Horaxis and the Customer. It reflects the parties' commitment to processing Personal Data in accordance with GDPR and global data protection standards.

1. Roles and Scope

Customer acts as the Data Controller. Horaxis acts as the Data Processor. This DPA applies to all operational procurement records and system-generated analytics processed via ProcureAI.

2. Data Sovereignty & Standalone Deployment

Where Customer utilizes the Standalone On-Premise deployment, the Processor (Horaxis) does not take possession of, nor store, Personal Data. All data remains within the Controller's hardware environment and is subject to the Controller’s internal governance.

3. Technical and Organizational Measures

Horaxis maintains high-level safeguards including:

  • Encryption: AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.2+ in transit.
  • Access Control: Role-based access (RBAC) and MFA enforcement.
  • Audits: Regular vulnerability scanning and operational monitoring.

4. Sub-processors

Horaxis engages infrastructure providers (e.g., Cloudflare for edge security) only as necessary for service delivery. All sub-processors are bound by confidentiality and security obligations no less restrictive than those in this DPA.
