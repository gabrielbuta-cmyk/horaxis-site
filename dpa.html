Data Processing Addendum

1. Roles

The Customer acts as the Data Controller of all ERP-derived data. Horaxis acts as the Data Processor, only acting upon the data to facilitate procurement intelligence.

2. Isolation of Processing

ProcureAI is designed to be deployed in a Standalone Instance. In such configurations, the Processor (Horaxis) does not take possession of Personal Data, as all storage remains within the Controller's infrastructure.

3. Technical Measures

Horaxis warrants that ProcureAI supports AES-256 encryption, TLS 1.3 transit security, and MFA authentication, aligning with ISO 27001 best practices.
