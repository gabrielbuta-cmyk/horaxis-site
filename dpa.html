Data Processing Addendum (DPA)

This Data Processing Addendum governs processing of personal data in connection with ProcureAI services.

Role of Parties

Customer acts as Data Controller. Horaxis acts as Data Processor.

Processing Purpose

Processing occurs solely to deliver procurement intelligence services.

Security Measures

  • Encrypted transmission (TLS)
  • Access controls
  • Infrastructure protection via Cloudflare
  • Secure transactional email providers

Subprocessors

Cloudflare (infrastructure) Email providers (transactional delivery)

Data Subject Rights

Horaxis supports GDPR rights including access, correction, and deletion.
