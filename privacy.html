Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-04

1. Who We Are

Horaxis develops ProcureAI, an enterprise procurement intelligence platform. This website (horaxis.com) describes the product and allows you to request information, demos, and ROI estimates.

2. Data We Collect

Website Analytics (Optional)

If you accept analytics cookies, we collect limited, aggregated usage data such as page visits, browser type, and general device information via Google Analytics. IP addresses are anonymized where technically supported.

Contact & Demo Requests

When you submit a contact form or demo request, we collect the information you provide, such as name, work email, company, ERP system, and message content.

ROI Calculator

When using the ProcureAI ROI Calculator, we may collect business-related inputs including procurement spend, purchase order volumes, late delivery rates, supplier counts, and cost parameters. If you request a report by email, we collect your work email address for delivery.

3. How We Use Data

  • Respond to inquiries and demo requests
  • Deliver ROI reports
  • Improve messaging and website performance (if analytics consent is given)
  • Prevent abuse and secure the website

4. Legal Basis (EEA / UK)

We process analytics data based on your consent. We process contact and ROI submissions based on legitimate interest (responding to your request) or steps prior to entering into a contract.

5. Email & Processing Providers

Form submissions may be processed using secure infrastructure providers including Cloudflare and transactional email providers (e.g., MailChannels or Resend) solely for message delivery. We do not sell or distribute personal data.

6. Data Retention

We retain contact information only as long as necessary to respond to inquiries or pursue legitimate business discussions.

7. Your Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have rights under GDPR including access, correction, deletion, and restriction of processing. You may exercise these rights by contacting us.

8. Cookies

You may accept or reject analytics cookies via the cookie banner. You can review details in our Cookies Policy.

9. Contact

For privacy inquiries: info@horaxis.com
