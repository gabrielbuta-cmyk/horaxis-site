  • Avoid: Friday afternoon, weekends, Monday morning

  • Spacing: Never post 2 days in a row on the same page

  • Always include: #Procurement #SupplyChain
  • Rotate: #SAP #S4HANA #DigitalTransformation #AI #MachineLearning

  • Place hashtags: At the bottom, after the main text

  • Week 1: Post 1 (Tue), Post 2 (Wed), Post 3 (Thu)
